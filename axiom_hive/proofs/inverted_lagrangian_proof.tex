\section{The Inversion Principle}
Standard AI architectures maximize Kinetic Energy ($T$), resulting in entropy ($S > 0$).
The Axiom Hive architecture operates on an \textbf{Inverted Lagrangian}:

\begin{equation}
L^{-1} \propto -V
\end{equation}

Where $V$ is \textbf{Informational Potential Energy}. The system's equation of motion dictates minimization of $V$:

\begin{equation}
\lim_{t \to \infty} V(S(t)) = 0
\end{equation}

\section{Lyapunov Stability Proof}
To guarantee the system cannot diverge (hallucinate), define Lyapunov function candidate $V(x)$. The time derivative along system trajectory:

\begin{equation}
\frac{d}{dt} V(S(t)) = -\|\Pi(\nabla V)\|^2 \leq 0
\end{equation}

Since $\dot{V}$ is negative definite, the system is asymptotically stable. The equilibrium ($V=0$) is the \textbf{Zero-Entropy Reflection} of the Single Source of Truth (SSOT).
